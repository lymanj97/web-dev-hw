Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsps white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsps white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsps butter
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Combine milk with viegar in medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in large bowl.
  3. Whisk egg and butter into milk.
  4. Pour flour mixture into wet ingredients. Mix until no lumps.
  5. Heat large skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter on skillet, cook until bubbles appear on surface.
  7. Flip with spatula and cook until browned on the other side.
