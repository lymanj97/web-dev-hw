About Jordan's Cracker Sandwiches

Jordan chose to create this project on cracker sandwiches because he feels a real passion for cracker sandwiches. Something magical happens when you put pepperoni and cheese between two crackers. The amount of combinations that you can create with different meats, cheeses, and artisanal crackers is exquisite.

He has been creating cracker sandwiches for over 15 years! These sandwiches mean the world to him and a world without cracker sandwiches is not a world to live in. In the future, Jordan hopes to create the Lyman Cracker Sandwich Foundation to help those without cracker sandwiches in their lives. Please keep an eye out for the future fundraising.
