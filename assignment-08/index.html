How to Make a Cracker Sandwich

Materials needed:

# of Sandwiches Crackers Cheese Slices Pepperoni
1 Sandwich 2 1 1
2 Sandwiches 4 2 2
3 Sandwiches 6 3 3

Step 1


  • Gather your crackers, cheese, and pepperoni.


  • Take one cracker and put the slice of cheese on top of it.






Step 2


  • Put your pepperoni on top of the cheese.








Step 3


  • Put the top cracker on top of the pepperoni.


  • Enjoy!
