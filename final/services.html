Our Products

Currently, we only specialize in 100% soy wax candles. We use candle tins and ECO series candle wicks, both sourced from Candle Science. All of the sizes of our products come in 8 oz. sizes and are not colored. Birchbark Candle Co. makes candles of many scents, but our most popular are:

  • White Sage and Lavender
  • Vanilla
  • Lavender
  • Cedarwood
  • Spiced Honey and Tonka
  • And of course, White Birch!

    • When for sale, our candles retail for $8, but we are always willing to barter and make a deal!
